PETROL PLANNER


Hi, thanks for visiting our site.


"With great power comes great responsibility"


Owning a vehicle is one such power and to do it responsibly is any rider's duty. One of the biggest responsibilities of a rider is to fuel his/her vehicle responsibly. The task is easier said than done as there are a lot of factors affecting said fueling.

For this reason we have decided to build an app that does the thinking for you. We call it the Petrol Planner. The main functionality of the app is that it suggests the optimal amount of petrol to be filled and the optimal frequency for the user. It does so according to the details of the user and is thus extremely personalized.
